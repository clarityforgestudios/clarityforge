The Visibility Gap in Energy

The public conversation about energy is being shaped by people with distribution strategies, not domain expertise. AI-generated content and misinformation campaigns have built-in production systems. The people who actually understand the grid, policy, and markets don't.

The problem isn't that experts are silent. It's that expertise alone was never built for content production. Without systems, a team, or dedicated time, even the most credible voice stays invisible.

But this isn't a permanent condition. Every expert we work with already has the credibility. They just need the infrastructure to make it visible. That's a solvable problem.
