Common Questions

How much of my time does this require?

For podcast clients, you show up and record. We handle everything else. Most clients spend 1-2 hours per week on their media presence. For executive visibility, we batch film 4-6 videos in one session, then produce and distribute them over the following weeks. Your time commitment is one session per month plus reviewing drafts.

How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

Marketing agencies build campaigns. We build infrastructure. A campaign runs for 90 days and stops. Our systems compound over time. We also specialize exclusively in energy, which means we understand the policy dynamics, the regulatory landscape, and the audience you're trying to reach.

I've tried content before and it didn't work.

Most experts who've tried content either did it all themselves and burned out, or hired a generalist agency that didn't understand their audience. We solve both problems. You focus on your expertise. We build the system. And because we know the energy sector, the content resonates with the right people from day one.

We're a small team. Is this realistic?

Our smallest engagement is $750/month. Our largest is $7,500+. We work with solo experts and firms with 20+ leaders. The Story Sprint exists specifically to figure out what makes sense before you commit to anything.

What does the first month look like?

Every engagement starts with a Story Sprint. From there, we define your system, set up your production pipeline, and begin publishing within 2-3 weeks. By the end of month one, you have a working rhythm and your first batch of content live.

Why can't I just hire a freelancer or give this to my marketing person?

You can, and many people try. The freelancer edits your audio and hands it back. Your marketing person adds it to the queue between the website update and the trade show booth. Neither of them understands energy policy, neither builds a system that compounds, and neither is thinking about how this episode connects to your LinkedIn strategy, your newsletter, your guest coordination, and your authority positioning six months from now. If all you need is editing, hire a freelancer. If you need a media system that builds credibility over time, that's a different thing.
