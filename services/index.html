Common Questions

How much of my time does this require?

For podcast clients, you show up and record. We handle everything else. Most clients spend 1-2 hours per week. For executive visibility, one filming session per month plus reviewing drafts. Your time commitment stays under 5 hours per month.

How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

Agencies build campaigns that run for 90 days and stop. We build systems that compound over time. We also specialize exclusively in energy, which means we understand the landscape and the audience from day one.

I've tried content before and it didn't work.

Usually that means you did it all yourself and burned out, or you hired a generalist who didn't understand your audience. We solve both. You focus on expertise. We build the system. Energy sector focus means the content resonates immediately.

We're a small team. Is this realistic?

Our smallest engagement is $400/month. We work with solo experts and firms with 20+ leaders. The Story Sprint exists to figure out what makes sense before you commit to anything.

What does the first month look like?

Every engagement starts with a Story Sprint ($500 individuals / $750 organizations) or Sprint+ ($750/$950 with a Voice Profile included). We define your system, set up production, and begin publishing within 2-3 weeks. 100% of your Sprint fee applies to your first month.

Why can't I just hire a freelancer?

A freelancer edits your audio and hands it back. They don't build a system that connects your podcast to your LinkedIn strategy, newsletter, guest coordination, and authority positioning. If all you need is editing, hire a freelancer. If you need a media system, that's what we do.
