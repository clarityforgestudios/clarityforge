Project Vanguard

The Client

Organization

Project Vanguard

Mission

Connecting military veterans to energy sector careers through community, content, and industry partnerships

Founder

Kevin Doffing, Army veteran and energy sector leader

Starting Point

Deep credibility and a clear mission. No content infrastructure, no public platform, no media presence.

The Challenge

Kevin had spent years building relationships across the energy sector and veteran community. He had a clear mission and real credibility. What he didn't have was any infrastructure to make that mission visible beyond his personal network.

No podcast. No newsletter. No LinkedIn strategy. No brand identity. The organization needed a media presence that matched the seriousness of its mission, built from scratch, in a voice that felt like Kevin: direct, plainspoken, and mission-driven.

What We Built

We built the entire media infrastructure for Project Vanguard from the ground up.

  • Brand identity and voice bible (direct, plainspoken, gritty, mission-driven)
  • Full podcast production pipeline: recording, editing, mastering, clips, distribution
  • LinkedIn content strategy and posting cadence for Kevin's personal profile
  • Short-form clip production optimized for LinkedIn, YouTube, and Facebook
  • Guest coordination system for recruiting energy and military leaders
  • Community leader amplification strategy across the PV network
  • Multi-platform distribution: podcast, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube

The Results

From zero

Built a recognized media platform from no existing audience or content infrastructure

Board-level

Advisory board members citing the podcast and content as a driver of organizational visibility

Partnerships

Industry partnerships formed directly through podcast visibility and guest relationships

Weekly

Consistent publishing cadence with full operational support from day one

100%

Client retention. Kevin has been a continuous engagement since the beginning.

Voice

A distinct brand identity that sounds like Kevin, not like a marketing agency

Where They Are Now

Project Vanguard is in continuous production with ClarityForge under an ongoing engagement. The podcast has become a recognized platform connecting veterans to energy sector careers, with partnerships, community events, and board-level visibility that didn't exist before the content infrastructure was built.

Kevin shows up and has the conversation. We handle everything else. The brand voice bible ensures every piece of content sounds like him, whether it's a LinkedIn post, a podcast intro, or a clip caption. The system runs because it was built to run.
