How We'll Operate

The working agreement.

Four operating principles that keep this clean on both sides.

Approval Workflow

Draft assumption (to be finalized together): Kevin reviews posts from his personal profile within 24 hours. PV page and other platform content publishes without review based on approved templates and Voice Profile. Open to adjustment.

Sensitive Content Protocol

Politically charged moments — major regulatory decisions, industry controversies, partisan flashpoints — are flagged for your direction before posting. The rest of the cadence continues uninterrupted.

Community Leader Cohort

Initial cohort of 5–8 leaders activated in Month 2–3, with additions each quarter based on performance and capacity. Leaders receive templates, posting cadence guidance, and approval workflow tools.

Coverage & Handoff

A dedicated executor (Carl or a new hire) owns day-to-day execution with my oversight. If either of us is out, the other covers critical moments. You won't go dark.

Before We Start

What we'll cover together.

A short working session to lock in the open questions. None of these need resolving in the proposal — they need resolving in conversation.

  1. The approval workflow that actually fits your bandwidth. Whether the 24-hour review on personal posts is right, or whether we want a lighter touch for some post types and a heavier one for others.
  2. The initial community leader cohort. Who are the first 5–8 leaders we activate in Month 2–3, and what topics does each one own?
  3. Admin access across platforms. Getting me and the dedicated executor into LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and Substack without creating any account-hygiene issues.
  4. Starting ad budget and guardrails. Whether we begin paid amplification at $500/mo or closer to $2,000/mo, and which organic post types are worth boosting first.
  5. The pre-funding question. Whether you want to start with the full scope or run the phased $1,500 option until funding fully closes.
