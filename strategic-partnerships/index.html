What a Month Looks Like for Your Team

We handle strategy, production, and distribution. Your leaders show up for the conversations.

1

Strategy Session

We align on priority narratives, upcoming opportunities, and which leaders speak to what topics this month.

2

Record

Each leader records 1-2 sessions. Formats include 1:1 expert conversations, solo briefings, policy breakdowns, and event recaps. 30-45 minutes per session.

3

Produce

We edit, master, create clips, write LinkedIn copy, build thumbnails, and prepare distribution packages for each leader's channels.

4

Distribute + Measure

Content publishes across LinkedIn, YouTube, and your priority platforms. We coordinate cross-posting, track engagement quality, and report on what's working.

Executive time commitment: 30-45 minutes per recording session, plus a quick review. We handle everything else. Strategy, production, writing, distribution, and measurement. That's 15-20 hours of work per leader that your team doesn't have to do.
