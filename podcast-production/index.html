How Each Episode Cycle Works

Most clients publish weekly or biweekly. Here's what each cycle looks like on your end.

Record

You have the conversation. We handle guest prep, topic framing, and scheduling.

Review

We edit, master, and produce clips. You review the final cut. About 15 minutes.

Publish

Episode goes live. Newsletter goes out. Clips distribute across LinkedIn, YouTube, and social.

Measure

We track what worked, share performance insights, and plan ahead.

Your time per episode: 30-60 minutes recording, 15 minutes review. We handle the other 15-20 hours of production, writing, and distribution.
