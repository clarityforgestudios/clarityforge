Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

ClarityForge Studios LLC is a media and authority-building agency based in Bend, Oregon. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

What We Collect

When you fill out a contact form on our site, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, organization (if given), the service you're interested in, and any details you share about your project. We only collect what you choose to give us.

Our website also collects basic analytics data through Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies, does not track you across sites, and does not collect personal data. It tells us which pages are visited and how visitors found us. Nothing more.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit through our contact forms to respond to your inquiry, schedule conversations, and provide the services you've requested. If we begin working together, we use your contact information to communicate about the engagement.

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not send marketing emails unless you've explicitly opted in.

How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are processed through the Wix form system and stored securely on Wix's infrastructure. Client communications and project files are managed through standard business tools (email, cloud storage). We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. The only parties who may access your data are the ClarityForge team members directly involved in responding to your inquiry or delivering services.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We use Plausible Analytics, which is cookie-free. If you interact with embedded third-party content (such as a Calendly booking widget or a YouTube video), those services may set their own cookies according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can request to see, update, or delete any personal information we have about you at any time. Just email us and we'll take care of it. We'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We won't make material changes without updating this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want to make a data request, contact us at info@clarityforgestudios.com or call (512) 569-6612.
